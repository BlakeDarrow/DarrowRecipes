Cilantro Lime Dressing
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
* 2 c fresh cilantro
* 1 garlic clove
* 1/4 c fresh lime juice
* 2 tsp maple syrup or honey
* 1/2 tsp ground coriander
* 1/2 tsp sea salt
* 1/2 c extra virgin olive oil
Make it Creamy
* 1 avocado
* 1/2 c whole milk greek yogurt
 
Prep
=========================================================
 

 
Directions
=========================================================
 
1. In a food processor, place the cilantro, garlic, lime juice, maple syrup, coriander and salt and pulse to combine.  With the blade running, pour in the olive oil and process until smooth.
2. Make it creamy: blend with one avocado or 1.2 c plain whole milk greek yogurt
