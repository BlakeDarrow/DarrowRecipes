Pumpkin Cheesecake Dip
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- 8 oz cream cheese, softened
- 8 oz whipped topping (cool while, heavy whipping cream)
- 3.4 c brown sugar
- 15 oz canned pumpkin
- 1 Tbsp pumpkin pie spice

 
Prep
=========================================================
 
Dipper Options: graham crackers, apples, vanilla wafers
 
Directions
=========================================================
 
Add softened cream cheese and whipped topping to a large bowl.  Using a hand held or stand mixer, while until well combined.  Add pumpkin, spice and brown sugar and mix well.  Top with additional spice and serve.
