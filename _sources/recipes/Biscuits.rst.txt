Biscuits
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- 2 cup flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 2/3 - 3/4 cup milk
 
Prep
=========================================================
 
None
 
Directions
=========================================================
 
Mix drys. Mix in grease, add milk.
Bake at 450 until browned.
