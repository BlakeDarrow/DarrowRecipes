Apricot Chicken
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- 2-3 lbs chicken breasts (thighs, canned chx, pork shops - meat of choice)
- 1 11ounce jar of apricot preserves, or preserves of your choice
- 1 tsp dried minced onion flakes
- 1 Tbsp dijon mustard
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce or coconut aminos
- 1/4 tsp ground ginger
- 1/4 to 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
 
Prep
=========================================================
 

 
Directions
=========================================================
 
1. Use a 4-6 qt crock pot.  Place the meat into you slow cooker.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the rest of the ingredients to make a sauce and pour over the chicken evenly.
3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours, or high for 4-6 hours.  If your chicken isn't frozen, will cook faster.  Serve with rice and veggies.
