Broccoli Chicken Pesto Bowl
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- 2 cups quinoa
- 4 cup chicken broth
- 2 broccoli crowns, cut into bite size pieces
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 4 green onions
- 1 pkg Simply Organic Pesto
- Parmesan
 
Prep
=========================================================
 

 
Directions
=========================================================
 
1. Make quinoa with chicken broth.
2. Saute broccoli with chicken breast.
3. Once cooked, add pesto package to broccoli and chicken.
4. Place quinoa in pasta bowl and top with broccoli chicken mix and garnish with chopped green onions and parmesan to taste.
