
DarrowRecipes
##############


How to use
==========

Navigate to the left side panel. You will find a search bar and a list of recipes. 

You can search for specific recipes or keywords within the search bar, or manually navigate to them by selecting the recipe title in the Recipes panel. Alternatively, use the list below.

You can also `submit <https://blakedarrow.github.io/DarrowRecipes/submit>`_ your own recipes!
