Pork Roast
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- Pork loin roast
- Granulated garlic
- rosemary
- thyme
- salt and peper
- olive oil
 
Prep
=========================================================
 
Preheat oven to 450
 
Directions
=========================================================
 
Coat pork loins in oil and rub spices on the loin. Bake for approximately 40 minutes.
