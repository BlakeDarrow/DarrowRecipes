Pork Roast 
########################################################### 
 
Ingredients 
========================================================= 
 
- Pork loin roast
- granulated garlic
- rosemary
- thyme
- salt and peper
- olive oil 
 
Prep 
========================================================= 
 
Preheat oven to 450 
 
Directions 
========================================================= 
 
Coat pork loins in oil and rub spices on the loin.  Bake for approximately 40 minutes.
