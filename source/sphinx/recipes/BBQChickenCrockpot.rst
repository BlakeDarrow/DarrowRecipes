BBQ Chicken Crockpot
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- 5-6 chicken breasts
- 2 cups cubed potatoes
- 1 cup chopped carrots
- 1/3 cup diced onions
- 2 Tbsp flour
- 2 tsp minced garlic
- 1 bottle BBQ sauce
 
Prep
=========================================================
 

 
Directions
=========================================================
 
Place all ingredients into a crockpot.  Cook for 6-8 hours on low. Feel free to add vegetables of your choice.
