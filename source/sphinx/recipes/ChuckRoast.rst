Chuck Roast
###########################################################
 
Ingredients
=========================================================
 
- Chuck Roast
- 1 or more thick sliced onion
- 1 small bottle of red wine
- Steak seasoning
 
Prep
=========================================================
 
None
 
Directions
=========================================================
 
Place onions in bottom of crockpot with the roast sitting on top. Sprinkle steak seasoning and wine on top.
Cook in the crockpot for 8 to 10 hours.
