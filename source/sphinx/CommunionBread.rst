Communion Bread 
########################################################### 
 
Ingredients 
========================================================= 
 
- 1 cup flour, sifted
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup water
- salt 
 
Prep 
========================================================= 
 
None 
 
Directions 
========================================================= 
 
Mix ingredients and roll in flour, shaping in a ball.

Press(roll) onto an oiled 9x12 baking sheet and roll into a thin layer. 

Score into pieces.

Bake at 425 until lightly brown.
